Frequently asked questions:

What are sessions?

A session is a visitor’s full journey on your site, including every page they visit. Every time a visitor comes to your site, we’ll record their journey and how they interact with your page. You can analyze this data in Heatmaps and Recordings.

How many sessions should I capture?

Many customers choose a plan which lets them capture every session on their site. However, you don’t have to capture every session. If you pick a smaller plan, we’ll sample your data, ensuring consistent coverage over the month.

Most customers tend to pick a plan which captures at least 80% of their site’s traffic.

What are pageviews?

Pageviews only apply to the Free Plan. We count a pageview when someone views a single page on your site.

How do I install Hotjar?

Installation involves copy and pasting your unique Tracking Code on the site you would like to track. This tracking code allows you to collect data from your visitors and send it back to Hotjar. We’ve created guides to help you install the Tracking Code on various platforms that you can find here.

What's the commitment?

With Hotjar, there is no contract to sign, and you can upgrade or downgrade between different plans at any time – directly from your account. If you choose to pay monthly, you can cancel your subscription at any time by downgrading to the free Basic plan.

Are the prices per site?

Yes. One plan allows you to capture data for one site. You can, however, use one plan for each of your subdomains (e.g., hotjar.co.uk / hotjar.es). If your organization owns multiple sites, or if you work with clients, you select a plan for each site you set up. You can downgrade and upgrade your site plans at any time.

Can I handle multiple sites with one account?

Yes. There is no limit to the number of sites you can manage in one account. All of your sites are accessible under one account, making it easy to manage multiple different sites.

What happens if I exceed the session limit on the site’s plan?

Hotjar works differently from other tools that capture session data. Instead of collecting data from the first 500 sessions and then once you hit your cap, Hotjar automatically distributes data collection throughout the day (what we call ‘sampling’). Of course, if you pick a plan that’s equal to the number of sessions on your site, you’ll collect all the data, and there will be no need for sampling.

Do you offer yearly price plans?

Yes – Hotjar gives you the option to pay for your plans annually. When annual payment is set up in-app with a credit card or Paypal, a 20% discount before VAT is added to your bill. Take a look at our help doc for further details.

What payment methods do you support?

For monthly payments, we support all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX, Maestro, and Discover) and Paypal. For yearly payments on the Business plan, we accept payments via wire transfer, all major credit cards, and Paypal.

Can I collect more sessions than what’s listed above?

We’d love to understand your specific needs. Reach out to a sales representative for a no-obligation consultation here.

I am a Nonprofit. Do you offer any discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts to a Nonprofit organization. Please contact us for more information.

What’s the cancellation policy?

All new accounts have a 30-day-money-back guarantee. Cancel within 30 days and you’ll receive a full refund.

If you cancel after 30 days you’ll be charged up until the end of the current billing cycle. For customers on a monthly contract, that will be a month after your last payment. For customers on an annual contract, that will be a year after your last payment.

Remember, you never need to cancel your Hotjar account completely, You’ll simply move down to our free forever Basic account. For more information see our cancellation policy.
