Your privacy & Hotjar

From day one, Hotjar was designed and built with privacy in mind. We wanted to help our costomers improve their websites and create better user experiences, while keeping privacy t the core of our software-and we've been doing it since 2014.

Today, Hotjar is used on over 900,000 websites in 180+ countries. We believe we have a responsibility to set the example and protect not just the privacy of our customers, but also that of people who visit their website -in other words:you.
